The family issue!

  • Rohit Kumbhar
  • July 28,2020
  • 1

It is not a big deal if you are successful and earning good money and all, but what matters the most is that how responsible you are towards your family and efficiently you are sorting out your family issues.

Either you belong to a middle class family or a upper class family, family issues are somethings we all have, and it’s not always related to money but mostly it is because of the following reasons...

family issues are somethings we all have

Expectations:

We all have certain expectations with others, we want the other person to be in a certain way and for this reason we keep on poking them in every little things we want from them and in long term it really gets irritated. For example imagine you getting up in morning at 8 AM and you parents keeps poking you every morning for the same thing and same lecture repeats every day and one day out of irritation you shouted on your parents and now they got hurt and the family drama begins. Another best example would be, you using your smart phone the whole day and your parents keeps instructing you to put the phone down, but it’s not always parents expectation for their children but sometimes children keep certain expectation from there parents.

Unfulfilled basic needs:

Besides Roti (food) ,Kapda (cloths), Makan (house) their exists 6 other basic human need which everyone tries to fulfill as explained by Tony Robbins:

  • Certainty: assurance you can avoid pain and gain pleasure
  • Uncertainty/Variety: the need for the unknown, change, new stimuli
  • Significance: feeling unique, important, special or needed
  • Connection/Love: a strong feeling of closeness or union with someone or something
  • Growth: an expansion of capacity, capability or understanding
  • Contribution: a sense of service and focus on helping, giving to and supporting others

If any of these basic 6 human needs are not fulfilled or fulfilled only partially, the person starts doing something or starts acting in a way to get the basic needs fulfilled, and if he is still not able to fulfill the need he became anxious and starts acting oddly with other family members.

Superiority and disbelieve :

This is something which probably everyone has experience , it is a situation when one family member is superior and whose decision is supreme but the other family member has disbelieve on the superior member and can’t put any objection and has to accept his decision, for short term its mostly fine but when they have to accept their decision every time for a long term then this often ends up at a conflict or the inferior one starts behaving oddly with the superior one.
